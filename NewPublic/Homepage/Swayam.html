About Us

Swayam Learning, with its vast experience in the field of broadcasting, system integration and education has designed CBSE,NCERT,GSEB, UP board curriculum with a perfect blend of fun, knowledge and learning to meet this challenge. Our focus is to inspire and empower each individual with relevant, reliable and rigorous approach towards education. CBSE/NCERT curriculum is available in Hindi and English and GSEB in Gujarati enabling students to learn in their preferred language.
